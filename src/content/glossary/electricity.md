Electricity is the flow of electrical energy carried by the movement of electrons through a conductor. It is a fundamental force of nature that exists in lightning, static discharge, and biological nervous systems. In practical terms, electricity is generated at power plants, transmitted through power lines, and delivered to homes and businesses where it powers lights, appliances, and electronics.
