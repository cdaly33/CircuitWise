Impedance (Z) is the total opposition to current flow in an AC circuit, measured in **ohms (Ω)**. It combines resistance (R) and reactance (X) into a single value. Impedance is to AC circuits what resistance is to DC circuits, accounting for both energy dissipation and energy storage effects.
