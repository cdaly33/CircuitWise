term: "Ampacity"
category: "fundamentals"

---

Ampacity is the maximum current a conductor can carry continuously without exceeding its temperature rating. It depends on wire gauge, insulation type, ambient temperature, and installation method. Exceeding ampacity causes overheating, insulation damage, and potential fire hazards.
