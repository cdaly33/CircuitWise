A multimeter is a handheld test instrument that combines a voltmeter, ammeter, and ohmmeter into one device. It measures voltage, current, and resistance, and often includes continuity testing. Digital multimeters (DMMs) are the standard tool for electricians and technicians, displaying readings on an LCD screen with high accuracy.
