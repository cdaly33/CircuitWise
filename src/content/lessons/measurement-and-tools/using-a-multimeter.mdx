## The Multimeter

A multimeter is the most essential tool for anyone working with electricity. It can measure voltage, current, and resistance.

### Parts of a Multimeter

- **Display** — Shows the measured value
- **Selection dial** — Choose what to measure
- **Probes** — Red (positive) and black (negative/common)

### Measuring Voltage

1. Set the dial to voltage (V)
2. Select AC or DC as appropriate
3. Touch probes to the two points you want to measure across
4. Read the display
