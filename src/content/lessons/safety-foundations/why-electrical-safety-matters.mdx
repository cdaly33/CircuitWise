## Why This Matters

Electricity is one of the most useful forces in modern life — but it can also be one of the most dangerous. Before we dive into the fascinating world of circuits, voltage, and current, we need to understand the risks.

### The Invisible Danger

Unlike fire or sharp edges, electricity is invisible. You cannot see it, smell it, or hear it until it's too late. This is what makes it especially dangerous.

### Key Safety Rules

1. **Never work on live circuits** unless you are trained and qualified
2. **Always assume a wire is live** until you have tested it yourself
3. **Use insulated tools** rated for electrical work
4. **Keep water away** from electrical equipment
