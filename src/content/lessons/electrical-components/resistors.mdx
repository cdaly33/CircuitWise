## What Is a Resistor?

A resistor is a passive component that opposes the flow of electrical current. Resistors are one of the most common components in any circuit.

### How Resistors Work

Resistors convert electrical energy into heat. The amount of opposition they provide is measured in **ohms (Ω)**.

### Reading Resistor Values

Resistors use a color band system to indicate their value:

- **Black** = 0
- **Brown** = 1
- **Red** = 2
- **Orange** = 3
- **Yellow** = 4
